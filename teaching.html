Teaching

In the classroom.

I am the recipient of the 2026 NACTA Doctoral Student Teaching Award, a national recognition. I served as teaching assistant for a synchronous graduate course in program evaluation — independently designing and delivering lectures and mentoring students through complete evaluation plans — and for an asynchronous graduate statistics course, where I used Perusall's collaborative-annotation analytics to monitor engagement and target support. I hold a Teaching with Virtual Exchange certificate, have completed professional development on AI in teaching at UF, and co-created an online module for a USDA-funded certificate program. I am prepared to develop graduate and undergraduate coursework on evaluation, applied statistics, and AI applications in agricultural education and communication.
