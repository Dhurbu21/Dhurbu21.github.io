About me

Evaluator. Quantitative Methodologist.
Social Scientist.

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Agricultural Education and Communication at the University of Florida (IFAS), with a minor in Research and Evaluation Methodology. My research sits at the intersection of program evaluation, human dimension, and empirical machine learning.

I design and analyze quasi-experimental and survey studies, applying structural equation modeling, supervised & unsupervised machine-learning–based causal inference to large datasets in education and Extension

I also build AI and NLP tools — chatbots, text-mining pipelines, and LLM agents — to extend what evaluation can do.
